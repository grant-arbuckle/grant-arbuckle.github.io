About Me

As a business management graduate, I never thought I would fall in love with programming and data.

I got my start out of college working in finance and investment banking, where I began working with
large datasets and sharpening the analytical side of my skillset.

From there, I transitioned to a retailer with an international presence as a financial reporting and
planning analyst. In this role I wore many hats, and had exposure to many different facets of the business.

Through this exposure, I saw numerous opportunities to automate manual processes,
produce improved data-driven insights for stakeholders, and really take the lead in utilizing
the power of programming and modern tools to bring about positive change in the company.
This led to my self-discovered love for Python and process automation.

In my current role I utilize MS SQL and leading data query & ETL tools to clean, migrate, and archive
legacy data for clients. Every day I grow more in my SQL knowledge!

I spend my free time learning more about Python, RPA, data analytics, and developing tools
to save time and money for clients.
